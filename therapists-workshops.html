Circle of Support: Learning & Practice

The next "training" or "clinical" part of the EFT Circle of Support every other month starting May will be based on EFT Trainer George Faller's Success in Vulnerability. George has a very particular way of approaching training and the goal of the program is to empower therapists by helping them maintain a sharper focus in session by learning to become more tactical, intentional, aware and fully engaged in the therapeutic process. Anyone who has ever experienced George's teaching knows that you will come away with a much clearer sense of what you're doing and how to do it in your couples sessions.

EFT Connection & Networking Meeting - New this year!

Every other month, you will have an opportunity to connect with your fellow EFTers in a supportive online environment. There will be three different ways that you can choose to connect through small groups (via breakout rooms). The topics are as follows:

  • Networking and the path to certification: Meet and talk with fellow clinicians to learn more about their practice and areas of expertise. Find potential referral sources. Ask questions about the process of recording, supervision, and the requirements for becoming a certified EFT therapist. Learn more about upcoming training opportunities and ways to get involved online.
  • Self of therapist support: Come to this group with your stuck spots and learning edges. Here is an opportunity to share what is challenging you as you learn this beautiful model. You are not alone!
  • Role playing/skills practice: A simple prompt will be provided to give you an opportunity to practice one or two of the core EFT skills. This is a great opportunity to get more comfortable with the skills introduced at the Externship and Core Skills.
